Frequently Asked Questions

What is Teeth - Dental Journal?

Teeth - Dental Journal is an iOS app designed to help you track and manage your dental health history. It allows you to record dental appointments, procedures, treatments, and maintain a complete record of your oral health journey all in one convenient place.

Is Teeth - Dental Journal free to use?

Yes, Teeth - Dental Journal is free to download and use. You can track all your dental appointments, procedures, and maintain your complete dental history without any subscription fees.

What information can I track with the app?

You can track a wide variety of dental information including: dental appointments, procedures performed, specific teeth affected, treatment notes, dentist information, dates of service, and any other details related to your oral health care.

Can I track multiple family members?

Currently, each app installation is designed for one person's dental records. If you want to track dental records for multiple family members, you can consider using separate devices or profiles.

Is my dental data secure and private?

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. All your dental health data is stored securely on your device. We do not collect, share, or sell your personal dental information to third parties.

Can I export my dental records?

The app is designed to keep your records easily accessible on your device. For specific export features or data portability questions, please contact our support team.

What iOS version do I need?

Teeth - Dental Journal requires iOS 15.0 or later. Make sure your device is running a compatible version of iOS for the best experience.

Can I set reminders for dental appointments?

Yes, the app includes reminder functionality to help you stay on top of your dental appointments and routine checkups, ensuring you never miss an important visit.

How do I backup my dental records?

Your data can be backed up through your device's standard iOS backup process via iCloud or iTunes. This ensures your dental records are safe even if you need to restore your device.

I found a bug or have a feature request. How can I report it?

We'd love to hear from you! Please visit our Support page to submit feedback, report bugs, or suggest new features. Your input helps us make the app better for everyone.
